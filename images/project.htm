What makes CHS different?

Our Founder, Troy Clark started the company 30 years ago and committed from day one to be your source for any need you have. As a Division of Community Home Services, we continue that commitment today under the ownership of John Kaplan and his two sons David and John. We want to be your go to partner for all our homes swimming pool and lawn needs.  Our goal is to enable you to live a better life.

We also invest in our employees, not contractors, as well as our state of the art technology, trucks, and equipment in order to provide the most consistent pool and lawn services in the area.  Ultimately, we want to earn your trust and allow Community Home Services to provide you Better Care for Better Living.
